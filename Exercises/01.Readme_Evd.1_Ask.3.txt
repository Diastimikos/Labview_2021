### Project: Thermometer & Tank Control
**Description:** Controls temperature and tank level with live indicators. Two switches control LEDs. Stop button halts the process.
**Controls:** Thermometer Control, Tank Control, Switch1, Switch2, Stop
**Indicators:** Thermometer, Tank, Meter, LED1, LED2
**Concepts:** While Loop, Controls, Indicators
